Where is my order?
Your parcel should be at your doorstep soon if you have ordered from us. Click the 'Track Groceries' button to see your order status. Click on 'Contact Support' if there is a problem with your delivery.

How can I cancel my order?
Cancellation of order is only allowed before the confirmation of the store. There should be a blue 'cancel' button if the store have not confirmed your order

How do I return/exchange broken and expired items?
A refund will be provided after we have confirmed a defect in the item. Please contact support by clicking 'Live Support' at the bottom of the page
